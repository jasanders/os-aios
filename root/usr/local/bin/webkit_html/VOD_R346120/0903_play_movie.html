Strong World

Rating: PGR
Runtime: 113 minutes
Released: 12 December 2009
Country: Japan
Genre: Animation, Science Fiction & Fantasy

Synopsis
Luffy and the team starts their trip in a forest which is full of monsters, but suddenly Nami disappeared leaving a message, and the team start to look for her, will they able find her or not .. 20 years after his escape from Impel Down, the legendary pirate Shiki, the Golden Lion, reappears causing massive upheaval to the Marines. During his long seclusion, he was able to come up with a scheme to bring the World Government to his knees. On his way to execute the plan, Shiki crosses paths with the Straw Hat Pirates and becomes so impressed with Nami's knowledge of meteorology that he abducts her to forcedly enlist her into his crew. Luffy and the gang end up on a strange land populated with monstrous beasts as they desperately search for Shiki and Nami.

Actor
Mayumi Tanaka: Monkey D. Luffy (voice), Kazuya Nakai: Roronoa Zoro (voice), Akemi Okamura: Nami (voice), Kappei Yamaguchi: Usopp (voice)

Director: Munehisa Sakai
