moServices on drive

Move moServices to plugged/embedded drive, as HDD of flash.

On enabling, the module scan drives and find EXT3 partition. If found, it makes the .mos directory, copy moServices to it and mount. If not found an EXT3 partition, it makes the .mos image with 512 Mb size on any other partition, copy moServices to it and mount.


After disabling the module, you must restart the player.


Removing the module is performed in three stages:

1. Disable module

2. Reboot

3. Remove module
